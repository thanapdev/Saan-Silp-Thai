Cultural Heritage
into Soft Power

Wickerwork is not merely a collection of household items, but a true art form passed down through generations. It serves as a representative of Thai folk wisdom, brilliantly transforming natural materials found in the surroundings such as bamboo, rattan, and bulrush into creative masterpieces that echo a traditional way of life.

Today, "Sann-Silp-Thai" proudly brings this branch of art back to vibrant life. We present it to the global community as an invaluable Soft Power, harmonizing ancient traditions with modern aesthetics.

The Charm of Natural Materials

Each natural element offers a distinct identity and tactile experience.

Bamboo

Highly flexible yet incredibly durable. Bamboo is popularly woven into intricate baskets, utility containers, and complex traditional fishing traps.

Rattan

Tough and exceptionally pliable. Rattan exudes a luxurious and meticulous feel, frequently shaped into premium furniture and high-end home decor.

Bulrush (Krajood)

The charm of Southern Thailand. Transformed into fashion bags and soft mats, bulrush offers a gentle texture and warm, natural hues.

Masterpiece Products

Exquisite craftsmanship rooted in local wisdom.

Kratib Khao (Northeastern)

A cylindrical bamboo container designed for sticky rice. Its brilliant double-walled woven structure acts as excellent thermal insulation, keeping the rice soft and warm all day long.

Ngob (Central)

The iconic Thai farmer's hat. Woven from palm leaves over a structural bamboo frame, it allows supreme airflow while efficiently protecting the wearer from harsh sun and rain.

Yan Lipao Handbag (Southern)

A pinnacle of Southern Thai luxury handicraft. Thin threads of climbing ferns are meticulously plaited into complex geometric patterns, often embellished with elegant gold or silver clasps.

Kong Khao (Northern)

A tall, elegant container used by Lanna people for sticky rice. Featuring a distinctive wooden cross base and a domed lid, it beautifully reflects the identity and lifestyle of the North.

Bamboo Basket (Central)

A versatile carrying basket used for daily shopping or temple offerings. Bound tightly with thick rattan along the edges, it is designed for immense strength and longevity.

Krajood Bag (Southern)

Bulrush sedge woven into contemporary fashion tote bags. Known for its distinctively soft texture, natural beige hues, and minimalist elegance.
