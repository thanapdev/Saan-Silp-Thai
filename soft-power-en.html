From Tradition to Global Fashion

From community hands to the eyes of the world.

Contemporary Design

The Modern Aesthetics of Runways and Interiors

Wickerwork has transcended its former boundaries. Once perceived merely as inexpensive utility goods, it has boldly stepped onto the fashion runways by both Thai and international designers.

Furthermore, it has become a staple in the interior design of 5-star luxury hotels and resorts worldwide, heavily featuring rattan chandeliers and wicker chairs to foster a relaxing yet sophisticated 'Luxury Tropical' atmosphere.

The Power on the Global Economic Board

Economic Impact (Generating Income for the Community)

The Soft Power movement is not just about image; it brings substantial revenue into the country, distributing wealth directly to the grassroots. Wickerwork has evolved into a high-value export industry. Direct support and investment in community wickerwork empower the elderly and villagers, equipping them with better livelihoods and sustainable careers.

Global Connection (Connecting Thailand to the World)

International luxury fashion houses and local Thai brands have begun exporting to European and American markets. Traditional Yan Lipao and rattan handbags are gracing the pages of prominent magazines and prominent fashion weeks. It's a profound cultural communication without words, seamlessly enchanting foreigners to fall in love with "Eco + Handmade" masterpieces.
